# Tai Chi Meditation Music Download Guide

This guide will help you download license-free (CC0/Public Domain) meditation music suitable for commercial use in your Tai Chi app.

## Required Files

Place 4-6 MP3 files in the `assets/audio/music/` folder with these suggested names:
- `tai_chi_peaceful_morning.mp3`
- `flowing_water_meditation.mp3`
- `mountain_breeze_zen.mp3`
- `inner_peace_relaxation.mp3`

## Best Sources for CC0 (Public Domain) Music

### 1. Pixabay Music (Recommended)
- **URL**: https://pixabay.com/music/search/meditation/
- **License**: Pixabay Content License (Free for commercial use, no attribution required)
- **How to Download**:
  1. Visit Pixabay Music
  2. Search for "meditation", "zen", "tai chi", or "relaxing"
  3. Click on a track you like
  4. Click "Download" button
  5. Rename the file and place in `assets/audio/music/`

### 2. Chosic (CC0 Section)
- **URL**: https://www.chosic.com/free-music/all/?sort=&attribution=no
- **License**: CC0 Public Domain
- **How to Download**:
  1. Visit the URL above
  2. Filter by "No Attribution Required"
  3. Look for calm, ambient, or meditative tracks
  4. Download MP3 files
  5. Place in `assets/audio/music/`

### 3. Free Music Archive
- **URL**: https://freemusicarchive.org
- **License**: Various (Filter for CC0 or Public Domain)
- **How to Download**:
  1. Search for "meditation" or "ambient"
  2. Use filters to select "Public Domain" or "CC0" license
  3. Download tracks
  4. Place in `assets/audio/music/`

### 4. Internet Archive
- **URL**: https://archive.org/details/audio
- **License**: Public Domain
- **How to Download**:
  1. Search for "meditation music" or "tai chi"
  2. Look for collections marked "Public Domain"
  3. Download individual tracks
  4. Place in `assets/audio/music/`

## Recommended Search Terms

When searching on these platforms, use:
- "meditation"
- "zen"
- "tai chi"
- "qigong"
- "ambient relaxation"
- "peaceful instrumental"
- "calm oriental"
- "chinese meditation"

## File Requirements

- **Format**: MP3
- **Duration**: 3-8 minutes per track
- **Quality**: 128kbps or higher
- **License**: CC0, Public Domain, or explicit commercial use permission

## Important Notes

1. **Always verify the license** before downloading
2. **CC0 and Public Domain** = No attribution needed, free for commercial use
3. **CC-BY** = Requires attribution (credit to the artist)
4. **CC-BY-NC** = Non-commercial only (NOT suitable for this app)

## After Downloading

1. Place MP3 files in `/assets/audio/music/`
2. Update the `practice_page.dart` file to reference your actual filenames
3. Test the audio playback in the Practice tab
